CarbonList

Mobile Eco-Friendly Shopping List.

I created a mobile web application - a shopping list. You can search and add groceries to your list, and for each item a carbon footprint is displayed. You can view the carbon footprint for each item or for the total sum of your list in different units, so instead of CO/kg, you can view it in kilometers by car - which is more easy to understand.

  • Date: January 2017
  • Client: KTH

ARmborst

Mobile Augmented Reality Game.

Created a mobile augmented reality game. Your cellphone is used as a crossbow and you can place out targets and shoot arrows on them. The further away you are from the target, the more points you gain for each hit.
